DOCUMENT 1: RETURNS AND REFUNDS POLICY
================================================================================

COMPANY POLICY: RETURNS AND REFUNDS
Last Updated: January 2026

1.0 Overview
Our goal is to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. This document outlines the eligibility and procedures for returns.

2.0 General Returns
Most items purchased from our store can be returned within 45 days of delivery for a full refund or store credit.

3.0 Return Process
To initiate a return:
- Log into your account
- Navigate to Order History
- Select the item and click "Return Item"
- Print the prepaid shipping label
- Ship within 7 days

4.0 Electronics Category
4.1 High-value electronic items, including smartphones, laptops, and tablets, are subject to stricter guidelines due to security and activation concerns.
4.2 Electronics must be returned within 15 days of delivery.
4.3 All original packaging, accessories, and documentation must be included.
4.4 Items showing signs of use beyond initial testing may be subject to restocking fees.

5.0 Refund Timeline
- Store credit: 1-2 business days
- Original payment method: 5-7 business days
- Bank processing may add additional time

DOCUMENT 2: USER ACCOUNT SECURITY GUIDE
================================================================================

USER ACCOUNT SECURITY GUIDE
Version 2.1

Section A: Account Creation
Users must provide a valid email address and a password containing at least 10 characters, including one special symbol.

Section B: Password Recovery
If you lose access to your account, you must perform a recovery. Security Guide: Users can reset passwords via the 'Forgot Password' link on the main portal login page.

Step 1: Click the link.
Step 2: Enter your registered email.
Step 3: Follow the email instructions sent to your inbox.
Step 4: Create a new password meeting the security requirements.

Section C: Two-Factor Authentication
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced security:
- SMS verification
- Authenticator app (recommended)
- Backup codes for emergency access

Section D: Account Security Best Practices
- Never share your password
- Use unique passwords for each service
- Enable login notifications
- Review connected devices regularly
- Log out from shared computers

DOCUMENT 3: SHIPPING AND LOGISTICS FAQ
================================================================================

SHIPPING AND LOGISTICS FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Where do you ship?
A: We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states, Canada, and selected international locations.

Q: Do you offer international shipping to Europe?
A: Yes, international shipping is available to all EU countries and the UK. To simplify the process for our European customers, EU and UK orders incur a $25 flat rate shipping fee which covers standard customs handling.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard domestic shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) is available for an additional fee. International orders typically take 10-14 business days.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes, tracking information is sent via email once your order ships. You can also view tracking in your account dashboard.

Q: What if my package is lost or damaged?
A: Contact customer support within 48 hours. We will file a claim with the carrier and either reship or refund your order.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Orders over $50 qualify for free standard domestic shipping. Members of our loyalty program receive free shipping on all orders.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATION SHEET: MODEL X100

Product Name: X100 Portable Power Station
Release Date: November 2025

Technical Specifications:
- Battery Type: Lithium-ion (Li-NMC)

Safety Features:
- Overcharge protection
- Short circuit protection
- Temperature monitoring
- Low voltage cutoff
- Surge protection

Warranty:
2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Battery capacity guaranteed to maintain 80% capacity after 500 charge cycles.
